## Product Overview
Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) synthetic rubber waterproofing membrane. This premium, single-ply elastomeric rubber sheeting is compounded with high-grade vulcanized EPDM polymer, carbon black, processing oils, and vulcanizing agents. It offers superior durability, extreme elasticity, and virtually unmatched resistance to weathering, ozone, and heat aging.

## Key Benefits
- **Extreme Elasticity**: Outstanding elastomeric performance with high elongation (>= 450%) and excellent structural recovery.
- **Longevity**: Exceptional resistance to UV radiation, atmospheric ozone, and aggressive thermal aging over a long service life.
- **Wide Temperature Range**: Broad temperature resistance, remaining highly flexible at extreme low temperatures (-45 deg C).
- **Chemical Resistance**: Excellent resistance to chemical substances, root penetration, acids, and alkalis.
- **High Strength**: High tensile strength and tear resistance for demanding civil engineering structures.

## Installation Method
1) Fully Adhered: Apply bonding adhesive to both the substrate and the sheet backing, then press the membrane down and roll. 2) Overlaps: Seal laps using EPDM seam tape and specialized primer. 3) Loose-Laid and Ballasted: Lay sheets loose and weigh down with river stones or concrete slabs.
