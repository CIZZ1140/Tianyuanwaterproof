## Product Overview
Building Roof Flexible PVC Waterproofing Membrane is a high-grade polymer sheet produced by Weifang Tianyuan from premium polyvinyl chloride resin, plasticizers, stabilizers, and UV absorbers. Reinforced with polyester fabric (L) or glass fiber (G), this membrane provides a lightweight, durable, and highly weather-resistant single-ply roofing solution with heat-welded seams.

## Key Benefits
- **Exceptional Weather Resistance**: Exceptional weathering, ozone, and UV resistance, allowing for direct, long-term exposure on industrial roofs.
- **Monolithic Seam Security**: Hot-air weldable lap joints create a homogenous, permanent, and monolithic seam that is stronger than the membrane itself.
- **High Mechanical Strength**: High mechanical strength, tear resistance, and outstanding resistance to root penetration (ideal for green roof systems).
- **Dimensional Stability**: Pliable formulation backed with polyester fabric (L) or glass fiber (G) for superior dimensional stability.

## Installation Method
Can be installed using three main methods: 1. Mechanically Attached System (for exposed metal/concrete decks): Lay the membrane flat, secure it with steel plates and fasteners along the seam line, and hot-air weld the overlapping sheets. 2. Fully Adhered System (for concrete roofs/substructures): Apply PVC adhesive to both the substrate and membrane underside, roll flat, and press with a roller. 3. Ballasted System: Lay loose, weld all joints, and cover with gravel. Note: All seam laps must be hot-air welded at 350°C–450°C.

## Storage & Shelf Life
