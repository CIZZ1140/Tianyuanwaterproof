## Product Overview
A multi-layer high-polymer composite waterproofing membrane. It is manufactured by laminating a core film of dense polyethylene (PE) between two layers of high-strength polypropylene (PP) non-woven fleece. Produced on a fully automated continuous extrusion laminating line, it forms a composite rolling material with excellent waterproofing and breathability.

## Key Benefits
- **Mechanical Reinforcement**: High tensile strength and mechanical resistance due to multi-layer reinforcement.
- **Breathable Protection**: Excellent waterproofing combined with good vapor permeability (breathable membrane).
- **Wet Application**: Can be applied directly on damp or wet substrates using cement-based adhesive, reducing the construction period.
- **Potable Water Safe**: Non-toxic, odorless, and perfectly safe for potable water reservoirs.
- **Durability**: Good low-temperature flexibility and long service life with thermal aging stability.

## Installation Method
Bonded to the substrate using a polymer cement mortar (prepared with cement, water, and specialized polymer dry powder adhesive). Clean the substrate, apply the prepared mortar evenly, roll out the membrane, and press firmly using a steel trowel or scraper to squeeze out air bubbles. Overlaps are sealed with specialized sealing tapes.
