## Product Overview
A pre-applied high-density polyethylene (HDPE) self-adhesive waterproofing membrane. Consists of a high-density polyethylene base sheet, a non-bitumen environmental high-polymer self-adhesive layer, and a protective layer (available with a white/yellow sand finish or an adhesive PET release film). This product is specifically designed for underground structures, subway stations, and civil foundation projects. It forms a continuous, permanent chemical bond with poured concrete, effectively eliminating lateral water migration.

## Key Benefits
- **Lateral Water Tracking Prevention**: Prevents lateral water tracking by establishing a continuous, permanent chemical bond with the cast-in-place concrete as it cures.
- **Superior Mechanical Performance**: High mechanical performance, including exceptional tensile strength, elongation, and superior tear and puncture resistance.
- **Corrosion Resistance**: Excellent resistance to acids, alkalis, salts, and other corrosive underground environmental substances.
- **Dimensional Stability**: Good low-temperature flexibility and outstanding dimensional stability across varying construction conditions.
- **Safe Installation**: Safe, quick, and flame-free cold-applied installation with a reliable self-adhesive overlap selvage.

## Installation Method
Pre-applied (blind-side) waterproofing method. Roll out the HDPE membrane onto the prepared substrate (mud slab or retaining wall) with the self-adhesive/sand layer facing up (towards the concrete pour). Lap joints must be tightly bonded using the self-adhesive selvage and rolled firmly. Position reinforcement rebars directly over the membrane. Pour the concrete mix; as it cures, a permanent mechanical and chemical bond is established between the membrane and the concrete.
