# High-Performance Liquid-Applied Waterproofing

Polyurethane (PU) waterproof coating is the ultimate solution for seamless, joint-free protection. By curing into a tough, rubber-like elastic film, it provides an impenetrable barrier that perfectly conforms to the most irregular substrates and complex pipeline penetrations.

Ideal for high-end residential basements, commercial kitchens, and large-scale civil engineering, Tianyuan's PU coating offers extreme tensile strength and low-temperature flexibility. As a factory-direct manufacturer, we provide both single and two-component systems with full OEM support for drum branding and color customization.
