## Product Overview
TFY Self-adhesive Bitumen Waterproof Membrane is a high-polymer "peel-and-stick" cold-applied waterproofing sheet. Manufactured from advanced SBS rubberized bitumen, it is surfaced with a reflective aluminum foil shield, cross-laminated PE film, or sand, and protected on the adhesive side by a siliconized release film. It offers instant bonding, high elasticity, and self-healing properties, ensuring supreme watertight performance without open flames.

## Key Benefits
- **No Open Flame Required**: Cold self-adhesive installation eliminates fire hazards, smoke pollution, and safety risks on-site.
- **Exceptional Bonding Strength**: High-polymer adhesive compound bonds instantly and permanently to concrete, metal, wood, and PVC surfaces.
- **Self-Healing Properties**: Bitumen adhesive naturally flows to seal small nail penetrations, minor cuts, and punctures.
- **Reflective Thermal Shield**: Aluminum foil surface minimizes solar heat absorption, protecting the bitumen from UV degradation and reducing cooling costs.
- **High Elongation & Flexibility**: Accommodates substrate cracks and thermal movements perfectly.

## Installation Method
Peel-and-stick cold application. Substrates must be structurally sound, dry, clean, and free of dust or oil. For porous or dusty concrete surfaces, apply a bitumen primer and let it dry. Align the membrane roll. Peel off the siliconized release film from the adhesive side, then press the membrane onto the substrate. Smooth firmly from the center outwards to eliminate air pockets. Run a heavy steel hand roller over all overlap joints to achieve an airtight, leak-proof seal. Installation should be performed above 5°C.

## Packaging & Loading

## Storage & Shelf Life
Must be stored vertically on flat, dry, shaded ground. Protect from direct sunlight, rain, extreme heat, and sparks. Ambient storage temperature should not exceed 45°C. Stack rolls only in a single layer to prevent core deformation. Shelf life is 12 months.
