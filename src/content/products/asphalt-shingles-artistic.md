## Unleash Architectural Creativity

Designed for specialty roofs and artistic buildings, our Hexagonal and Fish-Scale shingles offer a unique pattern that sets a project apart. These shingles are particularly well-suited for steep roofs where the pattern remains clearly visible from the ground.

### Design Highlights
- **Unique Patterns**: Hexagonal tiles provide a geometric modern look, while Fish-scale offers a classic, vintage feel.
- **High Flexibility**: Easily adapts to domes, conical roofs, and complex architectural shapes.
- **Superior Waterproofing**: Full-back adhesive ensures a complete seal against moisture ingress.
- **Vivid Color Options**: Available in single or variegated colors to match any design palette.
