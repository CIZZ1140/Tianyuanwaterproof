## High-Performance SBS/APP Modified Bitumen Membrane

Weifang Tianyuan's SBS/APP modified bitumen membrane is a state-of-the-art waterproofing material designed for the most demanding construction environments.

### Benefits
- **High Heat & Corrosion Resistance**: Formulated to withstand extreme temperatures and harsh chemicals.
- **UV Resistant**: Protects the structure from damaging solar radiation.
- **Anti-aging**: Long-lasting performance with minimal maintenance.

### Specifications
- **Thickness**: 3.0mm / 4.0mm
- **Length**: 10m / roll
- **Surface Options**: PE Film, Aluminum Foil, Sand, or Mineral granules.

### Applications
- **Roofs**: Both flat and sloped commercial or residential roofs.
- **Tunnels**: Providing a reliable barrier in civil engineering.
- **Underground Projects**: Ideal for basements and foundations.
