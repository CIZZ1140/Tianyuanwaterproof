## Professional Torch Equipment for Waterproofing Contractors

The TY-SHIELD Professional Propane Roofing Torch is the industry-standard tool for high-performance torch-applied waterproofing. Designed for professional roofing contractors, this heavy-duty torch provides the consistent, intense heat required for proper fusion bonding of SBS and APP modified bitumen membranes.

We provide wholesale supply of torch kits to building material distributors and construction firms worldwide. OEM labeling and custom configurations for burner sizes and hose specifications are available for container-load orders.
