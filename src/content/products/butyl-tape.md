## Product Overview
Roofing Repair Sealant Tape Butyl Waterproof Tape is a highly flexible, non-hardening sealant tape composed of 100% solid-state unvulcanized butyl rubber compound laminated to a protective, heavy-duty embossed aluminum foil. It delivers permanent flexibility, excellent UV block, and immediate high-tack leak sealing across a wide variety of construction substrates.

## Key Benefits
- **Lifetime Elasticity**: Lifetime non-curing compound maintains constant elasticity and 100% solid content with zero shrinkage.
- **Extreme Weather Resistance**: Excellent low-temperature resistance down to -25°C, preventing brittleness or delamination in freezing conditions.
- **UV & Physical Barrier**: Embossed aluminum backing provides a robust barrier against weathering, physical wear, corrosion, and extreme UV rays.
- **Instant High-Tack Seal**: Instant water-tight seal upon application with aggressive high-tack adhesion on wood, concrete, glass, steel, and RV surfaces.

## Installation Method
Peel-and-stick cold application. Ensure the substrate is clean, dry, and free of dirt, rust, or loose paint. Cut the tape to the required length. Peel away the release film and carefully align the tape over the seam or crack. Press firmly along the entire surface of the tape using your hand, cloth, or a small roller to expel air bubbles and ensure 100% surface contact. Best results are obtained when applied at temperatures above 5°C.
