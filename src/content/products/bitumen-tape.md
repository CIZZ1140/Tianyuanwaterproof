## Product Overview
Bitumen Self-Adhesive Waterproof Sealing Aluminum Tape is a cold-applied flashing tape composed of polymer-modified asphalt laminated with a heavy-duty, anti-UV reinforced aluminum foil. Engineered for quick repairs, it provides immediate high-grab waterproofing and seam-sealing for residential and commercial structures.

## Key Benefits
- **Fast & Easy Repair**: Fast, cost-effective leak repair with immediate high-grab self-adhesive bonding, reducing labor costs.
- **UV & Thermal Protection**: Reinforced aluminum foil surface offers strong anti-UV protection and solar heat reflection to protect the bitumen layer.
- **High Temperature Stability**: Highly stable formulation that does not flow or drip at temperatures up to 80°C.
- **Anti-Corrosion Barrier**: Serves as an effective anti-corrosion barrier when wrapped around metal pipelines or steel roofing structures.

## Installation Method
Peel-and-stick cold application. Ensure substrate is clean, dry, and sound. Dusty concrete surfaces must be pre-treated with a bitumen primer first. Peel off the release lining from the adhesive side, align the tape, and apply it directly onto the seam. Use a hand roller or cloth to smooth the tape from the middle outwards, ensuring no air bubbles are trapped. Press overlap edges firmly.
