## Product Overview
Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS) Modified Bitumen Waterproof Membrane is a high-performance, torch-applied waterproofing sheet manufactured by Weifang Tianyuan Waterproof Material Co., Ltd. It features a premium blend of SBS modified asphalt reinforced with a robust polyester or fiberglass carrier and is surfaced with PE film, sand, or mineral granules to deliver outstanding structural waterproofing.

## Key Benefits
- **Extreme Cold Resistance**: Outstanding low-temperature flexibility down to -25°C, preventing thermal cracking in cold climates.
- **High Temperature Stability**: High temperature stability up to 100°C, ensuring the asphalt compound will not flow or sag on sloped roofs.
- **Superior Mechanical Strength**: Superior tensile strength and elongation, offering exceptional resistance to structural movement, puncture, and tearing.
- **Long-Term Protection**: Thick barrier construction (3.0mm–4.0mm) providing heavy-duty mechanical protection and long service life exceeding 15 years.

## Installation Method
Requires torch-on application (hot-applied). Clean the concrete substrate, ensure it is completely dry, and apply a coat of bitumen primer. Unroll and align the membrane roll. Use a commercial propane gas torch to heat the underside of the roll and the substrate simultaneously. When the thin PE burn-off film melts and a thin layer of glossy molten bitumen appears, roll the membrane forward slowly, pressing down to create a full bond. Seal all overlap joints by hot-melting with a trowel.

## Storage & Shelf Life
Store rolls vertically in a dry, well-ventilated, indoor warehouse environment. Keep away from direct sunlight, rain, moisture, and any sources of heat or ignition. Do not double-stack pallets to prevent deformation of the rolls. Shelf life is 12 months under optimal storage conditions.
