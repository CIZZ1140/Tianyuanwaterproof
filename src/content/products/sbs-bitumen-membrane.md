## High-Performance Waterproofing Solution

Weifang Tianyuan's SBS/APP modified bitumen membrane is a state-of-the-art waterproofing material designed for the most demanding construction environments. Whether it's a high-rise concrete roof or a deep basement foundation, our torch-down membranes provide an impenetrable barrier against water ingress.

### Key Features
- **Extreme Durability**: Formulated with premium bitumen and modifiers to resist aging and UV exposure.
- **Superior Flexibility**: Maintains integrity even in low-temperature environments, preventing cracks.
- **Robust Protection**: The 4.0mm thickness variant offers unparalleled puncture resistance.

### Applications
- Residential and Commercial Roofing
- Underground Basements and Foundations
- Bridge Decks and Tunnels
- Water Reservoirs and Sewage Treatment Plants
