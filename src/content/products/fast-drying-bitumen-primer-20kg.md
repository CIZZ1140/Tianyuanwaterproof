## High-Performance Bitumen Primer for Professional Waterproofing

The TY-SHIELD Fast-Drying Bitumen Primer is a critical component of any professional waterproofing system. By sealing dust and porous surfaces, it ensures a monolithic bond between the substrate and the subsequent waterproofing layers, whether they are torch-applied or self-adhesive.

Available in standard export-grade steel drums, we offer wholesale pricing for large-scale infrastructure and commercial roofing projects. Private labeling and custom formulations are available for established distribution partners.
