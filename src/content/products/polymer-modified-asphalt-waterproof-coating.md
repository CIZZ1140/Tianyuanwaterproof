# TY-SHIELD™ Polymer Modified Asphalt Waterproof Coating

**Polymer Modified Asphalt Waterproof Coating** is a premium water-based waterproofing emulsion engineered for durability and ease of application. By integrating high-quality rubber modifiers and nano-scale fillers into a petroleum asphalt base, we have created a material that combines the traditional reliability of asphalt with modern polymer elasticity.

### Professional Grade Protection
The TY-SHIELD™ technology ensures that the rubber molecules form a dense, cross-linked network within the asphalt matrix. This results in a **seamless, joint-free waterproof membrane** that can stretch to accommodate building movements and bridge micro-cracks. It is a preferred solution for complex geometries where sheet membranes are difficult to install.

### Eco-Friendly & Safe
As a water-based product, it is non-toxic and VOC-compliant, making it safe for use in enclosed spaces like basements and tunnels. The **cold-applied system** eliminates the fire hazards associated with torch-on membranes, ensuring a safer job site for contractors.

### Key Technical Advantages
*   **750% Elongation**: Superior flexibility to handle structural shifts.
*   **-20°C Low-Temp Flexibility**: Remains elastic even in harsh winter climates.
*   **Monolithic Seal**: 100% seamless application on pipe roots and corners.
*   **Excellent Chemical Resistance**: Resists soil acids and alkalis for long-term foundation protection.
