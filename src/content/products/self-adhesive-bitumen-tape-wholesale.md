## High-Performance Bitumen Sealing Solution

Self-adhesive bitumen tape is a premier, cold-applied waterproofing material engineered for high-performance sealing and repair. Combining a flexible SBS-modified bitumen compound with a durable aluminum foil top layer, it provides an instant, airtight barrier against water ingress.

Ideal for flashing, pipe joints, and emergency leak repairs, this versatile tape bonds effortlessly to concrete, metal, wood, and traditional roofing felts. As a direct factory supplier, Weifang Tianyuan supports global B2B clients with customized tape widths, lengths, and private label packaging to meet specific regional construction needs.
