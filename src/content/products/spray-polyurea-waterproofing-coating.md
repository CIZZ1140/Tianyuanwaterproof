# TY-SHIELD™ PolyArmor Spray Polyurea Waterproofing

**Spray Polyurea Waterproofing Coating** represents the pinnacle of modern liquid-applied membrane technology. It is a 100% solid, two-component elastomer system that delivers an impenetrable shield for the world's most demanding infrastructure projects.

### The "Shield of Armor" Technology
Using our proprietary **TY-SHIELD™ PolyArmor formula**, we have engineered a coating that isn't just a barrier—it's a structural reinforcement. Within seconds of leaving the spray gun, the isocyanate prepolymer and amino-terminated resins react to form a dense, cross-linked monolithic skin. This "Instant-Set" capability allows for rapid deployment on vertical walls, tunnels, and complex geometries with zero risk of sagging.

### Extreme Performance in Any Climate
While traditional coatings are sensitive to temperature and humidity, our **Pure Polyurea** series is designed to be moisture-insensitive during construction. It maintains exceptional physical properties from the freezing temperatures of Arctic subways (-45°C) to the intense heat of desert petrochemical facilities (+120°C).

### Engineered for Infrastructure
*   **100% Solid & Zero VOC**: Sustainable and safe for enclosed tunnel environments.
*   **20+ MPa Tensile Strength**: Capable of withstanding immense mechanical stress.
*   **1150 mg Abrasion Resistance**: Outperforms epoxy and rubber for heavy-duty traffic.
*   **Acid, Alkali & Chloride Resistance**: Essential for marine and chemical industry protection.
