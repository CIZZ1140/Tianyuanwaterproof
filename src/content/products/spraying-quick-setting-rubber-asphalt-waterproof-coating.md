# TY-SHIELD™ Spraying Quick-Setting Rubber Asphalt Waterproof Coating

**Spraying Quick-Setting Rubber Asphalt Waterproof Coating** is a state-of-the-art, two-component waterproofing system designed for high-efficiency infrastructure protection. By combining a proprietary synthetic rubber emulsion (Component A) with a precise coagulant agent (Component B), it creates an instant-set liquid membrane that redefined speed on the job site.

### The "5-Second" Gel Advantage
Unlike traditional liquid coatings that take hours to dry, our **TY-SHIELD™ technology** allows the coating to gel within 5 seconds of leaving the spray nozzle. This enables contractors to apply thick layers on vertical walls, complex corners, and even overhead surfaces without any sagging or runoff.

### 1000% Elongation: Engineered for Movement
Modern infrastructure is subject to constant vibration and thermal expansion. This coating is engineered with a core-shell structure that maintains rubber as the continuous phase, providing an incredible **1000% elongation at break**. It effectively bridges cracks and absorbs structural shifts that would rupture ordinary coatings.

### Monolithic & Self-Healing
Once applied, it forms a monolithic, seamless membrane that acts as a unified barrier. Its unique visco-elastic properties provide **nail-rod self-healing** capabilities, ensuring that minor punctures or fastener penetrations during subsequent construction phases do not lead to leaks.

### Global Project Support
Tianyuan supports large-scale projects with bulk barrel supply, comprehensive technical documentation, and **OEM branding services**. Whether for subways in Southeast Asia or bridge decks in North America, our spraying asphalt system provides the reliable, fast-track waterproofing solution required by modern engineering.
