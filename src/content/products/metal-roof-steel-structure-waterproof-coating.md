# TY-SHIELD™ Metal Roof Acrylic Waterproof Coating

**TY-SHIELD™ Metal Roof Acrylic Waterproof Coating** is a specialized, high-elasticity waterproofing system engineered to meet the unique challenges of steel structures and metal roofing. Formulated with 100% pure acrylic polymers and advanced weather-resistant fillers, it provides a seamless, "cool roof" solution that excels in extreme climates.

### Advanced Thermal Adaptation
Metal roofs are dynamic—they expand and contract with every sunrise and sunset. TY-SHIELD™ technology is designed to be highly elastic, maintaining its integrity even when the underlying metal panels shift. When used with our stitch-bonded polyester reinforcement, it creates a **durable, skin-like membrane** that masters the most vulnerable points of a roof: the seams, fasteners, and joints.

### Reflection & Protection
Beyond waterproofing, this acrylic system acts as a protective shield. Its high solar reflectivity bounces heat away from the building, preventing the metal from reaching high temperatures that accelerate aging and indoor discomfort. Additionally, it forms an airtight seal that isolates the metal from moisture, effectively **preventing rust and corrosion**.

### Engineered for Longevity
*   **350% Fracture Elongation**: Bridges gaps and absorbs structural movement.
*   **Wide Temperature Stability**: Stays flexible at -50°C and stable at 86°C.
*   **Monolithic Barrier**: Eliminates 100% of joint-related leakage risks.
*   **Anti-Corrosive Seal**: Stops oxidation at screws and gutters.
