## Product Overview
Traditional saturated organic asphalt roofing felt, commonly known as tar paper or linoleum underlayment. It is manufactured by fully saturating a thick, absorbent organic fiber base paper with premium molten petroleum asphalt. The surface is coated with talcum powder or fine sand to prevent sticking during unrolling, making it a reliable sloped roofing underlay.

## Key Benefits
- **Cost-Effective Underlayment**: Time-tested waterproofing and slip sheet underlayment for residential roofs.
- **High Saturation**: High asphalt saturation and oil absorption capacity for durable water resistance.
- **Temporary Protection**: Provides temporary waterproofing protection before the main roofing is installed.
- **Easy Handling**: Easy to handle, cut, and mechanically fasten onto plywood or wooden decks.
- **Vapor Permeability**: Allows vapor to pass while maintaining a secure liquid water barrier.

## Installation Method
Install horizontally starting from the low point of the pitched roof. Roll out the asphalt felt over the plywood/wooden deck. Overlap adjacent sheets by 2 to 4 inches at horizontal seams and 6 inches at end laps. Secure using galvanized roofing nails or heavy-duty staples. Install primary roof covering directly over the felt.
