## Engineered for Roof Protection

The TY-SHIELD™ Organic Asphalt Felt is a time-tested, reliable solution for residential and commercial roofing projects. Designed as a secondary waterproofing layer, it provides an essential barrier between the roof deck and the primary roofing material (shingles, tiles, or metal).

### Key Performance Advantages
- **Deep Bitumen Saturation**: Our advanced impregnation process ensures 100% saturation of the organic mat, providing exceptional water resistance and durability.
- **Micro-Porous Breathability**: The unique structure allows trapped moisture and vapor to escape from the wooden roof deck, preventing structural rot and mold growth.
- **Anti-Stick Talc Coating**: A premium talcum powder finish on the surface prevents the roll from sticking to itself, ensuring a smooth and fast unrolling process on-site.
- **High Tear Strength**: Reinforced organic fiber provides superior resistance to tearing during installation and extreme weather events.

### Ideal Applications
- Asphalt Shingle Roofing Underlayment
- Standing Seam Metal Roofs
- Clay and Concrete Tiles
- Garage Sheds, Villas, and Farmhouses
- Wood-Deck Structural Protection
