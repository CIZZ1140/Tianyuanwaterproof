## Essential Pitched Roof Underlayment

Asphalt saturated organic felt, often referred to as roofing felt or tar paper, is a time-tested material used as an underlayment in pitched roofing systems. By saturating a high-quality organic fiber mat with premium petroleum asphalt, we create a durable moisture barrier that is both flexible and vapor-permeable.

Primarily used under asphalt shingles, this felt protects the roof deck during construction and provides a critical secondary layer of defense against weather throughout the roof's life. As a specialized manufacturer, Tianyuan supplies export-quality asphalt felt conforming to international ASTM standards, supporting B2B clients with customized roll specifications and private label services.
