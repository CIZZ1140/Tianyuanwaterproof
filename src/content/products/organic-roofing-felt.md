## Petroleum Saturated Organic Roofing Felt

Our organic roofing felt is a traditional and reliable underlayment choice for residential roofing projects, providing a secondary barrier against water infiltration.

### Benefits
- **High Absorption**: Excellent capacity to absorb asphalt, ensuring a strong bond and waterproofing layer.
- **Secondary Waterproof Barrier**: Adds an extra layer of protection beneath asphalt shingles.
- **Excellent Flexibility**: Easy to handle and install even in cold weather.

### Specifications
- **Weight**: Available in 15lb and 30lb variants.
- **Material**: Premium organic paper saturated with high-grade petroleum asphalt.

### Applications
- **Underlayment**: Specifically designed as an underlayment for asphalt shingles.
- **Residential Roofs**: Ideal for pitched residential roofing systems.
